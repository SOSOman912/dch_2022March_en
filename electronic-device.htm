Since its opening, ahaa has been committed to providing a good reputation for products and services to gain support from consumers. Electrolux UltimateTaste Released the Kitchen Appliances’ Series

The Electrical Appliances Division and Electrolux co-organised the product launch event for the UltimateTaste kitchen range in October 2021. On the day of the event, the Deputy Consul General of the Swedish Consulate in Hong Kong, Mr Joakim Ladeborn, was invited to attend. Celebrity guests Amanda S and Vicky Cheng, Executive Chef of one-Michelin-star restaurant, also attended the event and gave an on-site cooking demonstration of the functions of the new built-in SteamPro steam oven and built-in SensePro induction cooker. There was also a product display area and a simulation area for trying the SteamPro steam oven in the venue, allowing the media and guests to experience the intuitive design and different functions of the UltimateTaste kitchen range, and thoroughly indulge in its revolutionary cooking experience.
