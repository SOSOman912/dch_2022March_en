Auriga’s Medical Information Exchange Platform Officially Launched

Auriga is actively involved in the development of the Greater Bay Area (GBA) and has successfully completed the cross-boundary logistics of multiple drugs. In order to further assist international pharmaceutical companies to introduce more new drugs into GBA, and even expand the business in GBA, Auriga has had active discussions with several international pharmaceutical companies and all parties aligned with the following two strategies:

  1. In-depth research on the Greater Bay Area’s healthcare system;
  2. The clinical experience sharing across healthcare professionals in Hong Kong, Macau and GBA cities on the use of new drugs and follow-up of patients' medications

Due to the current impact of the pandemic, the opportunities for healthcare professionals to conduct interactive exchange meetings are being hindered. To bridge this gap, Auriga has established an online and offline medical information exchange platform for healthcare professionals in the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area. Through the sharing and practice of clinical experience, they will have better treatment options, thereby improving the efficacy of treatment.   3. With both HK FB coverage + China WeChat coverage
  4. China WeChat with Chat Board function and linked DCHA Med Rep

Auriga (Malaysia)
2022 Annual Business Kick-off Meeting

In mid-January this year, the management of Auriga (Malaysia) held a two-day business kick-off meeting, during which some 40 representatives from different departments were invited to participate. At the meeting, the management not only reviewed the work performance and achievements in the past year, and also gave an outlook of this year's business development and the targets to be set. Representatives at the conference also have business sharing and product updating so that colleagues could better understand the company's strategy and goals for development this year.

Auriga Overseas Applied SFDC Principal Content Management System

Auriga Overseas has successfully rolled out Principal Content Management System tool offered by Salesforce.com (SFDC) across all DCH Auriga Operations. SFDC helps the operating department to strengthen information management, centrally integrate information related to all business development activities, and help capture different opportunities pipeline in the market. The system’s platform utilizes cloud based technology, which can convert a large amount of real-time information and data into different forms of charts and reports, providing quick feedback and assistance in the business decision-making process. In addition to providing real-time information and convenience to colleagues, cloud based technology can also reduce expenditure spent on hardware upgrading for departments.
