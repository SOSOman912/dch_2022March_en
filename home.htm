Lunar New Year Message from the CEO, Mr. Frank Lai

Dear Colleagues,

As we welcome the Year of the Tiger, I wish you all a very happy new year on behalf of Dah Chong Hong.

With the Year of the Ox behind us, I first want to thank everyone for your patience and endurance as we faced the ongoing challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic. During the year, our businesses dealt with supply chain disruptions, rising transportation costs and ongoing restrictions in tourism, shopping and dining. Together, we worked hard to adapt to changing circumstances, capture emerging opportunities and move forward with strategic developments. As a result of your dedicated efforts, we are well-positioned to benefit from recovering markets and I’m pleased to share that we were able to deliver a solid performance for the year.

Despite the ongoing uncertainties, we have begun to unlock the power of “One DCH” to build a more competitive and resilient company. DCH is a unique organisation with a rich heritage and a deep understanding of our local customers. There is no doubt that we have the raw materials to succeed in highly competitive and quickly evolving consumer markets, but long-term success cannot be achieved by standing still.

As “Project Blue Ocean” gathers momentum, we are strengthening our technological infrastructure, streamlining our operations and creating new opportunities for growth. In addition to widespread internal business enhancements, the launch of online-to-offline platforms like Ahaa and Hong Kong Motor City showcase a holistic approach to service delivery while initiatives like e-shopping joint venture, Home+, and Aurigamart in Singapore are helping us capture a greater share of the value chain. In every business stream, we are strengthening our fundamentals and developing new ways to leverage our end-to-end capabilities. With a focus on brand development, data utilisation and cross-functional synergies, we are building powerful and defensible platforms that are enriched by our culture of service.

Today, DCH is moving along a path towards a brighter, leaner and more robust future but our long-term success will depend on the commitment and contributions of our many team members. As we embrace the creativity and fresh enthusiasm of the Year of the Tiger, I’d like to take the opportunity to encourage you all to continue driving progress with innovative ideas, teamwork and perseverance. It is up to all of us, through contributions great and small, to lay the foundation for tomorrow and maintain the momentum for growth.

Even as we focus on our long-term operational fitness, workplace safety remains paramount. I encourage everyone to get fully vaccinated to protect their health, their colleagues and loved ones. With the ongoing Omicron outbreak, COVID-19 remains a real risk to our operations. It’s up to all of us to remain vigilant and adhere to protective measures, especially during the festive season.

I look forward to working with you all to build the DCH of tomorrow and hope you and your families enjoy a healthy, happy and prosperous Year of the Tiger.

Sincerely,
Frank Lai
Chief Executive Officer
Dah Chong Hong Holdings
