Celebrating the 100th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China

Constructs a Platform for Employees’ Development to Enhance Cohesion

DCH Motor has formed an "iron triangle" of three teams of party committees, labour unions and volunteers to jointly construct a platform for employees’ development so as to enhance cohesion, and facilitate operation and management; in the meantime, we adhere to the concept of "take from the people and use it for the people", treating public welfare as a corporate culture. Each colleague is encouraged to treat public welfare as a way of life, actively practice socialist core values, and assume corporate social responsibility.

The DCH Motor Party Committee takes the motto of “strengthening enterprises with party committee and pursuing excellence” as the central idea of the party’s building work, and promotes the party’s building work in the right direction; DCH Motor Union is positioned as a supporter of enterprises’ operations and a guardian of employees’ interests, and holds many interesting team-building activities for staff. Not only did they enrich the employees' amateur life, but also add colours to the operation and management of the enterprise.

Fun Day for Staff

Birthday Party for Staff

DCH Motor’s volunteering team has always cared about the children living in the mountains. In 2021, a 4S store will fund a wheatgrass plan and set up two DCH Motor’s wheatgrass classes. At the beginning of the new year, they will hold the ‘2022 Up! "Brush Adventures" of Wheatgrass Series of Activities’. The 100 children of wheatgrass class used a brush to draw a small wish of themselves in 2022. Each DCH Motor dealer helped a wheatgrass realize a wish. After completion, paintings of the wheatgrass class will be exhibited in different dealers.

DCH Motor will continue to carry out a series of activities with the wheatgrass class, and plan to arrange for employees and car owners to bring children of the same age to teach together, so that teenagers of the same age can interact with each other, influence each other, and practise with actions. DCH Motors hopes to not only provide materialistic help, but also spiritual care, bringing touch and transformation to the life of children living in the mountains.
