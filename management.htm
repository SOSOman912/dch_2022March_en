Actively Creates a Breastfeeding Friendly Environment

DCH was awarded a certificate of appreciation at the "Say Yes to Breastfeeding Recognition Ceremony 2021" by UNICEF HK in recognition of our support and promotion of breastfeeding-friendly workplaces.

DCH has been committed to implement various family-friendly policies to cater for the needs of different employees. In order to support breastfeeding, we introduced the "Breastfeeding Friendly Workplace" in our office building located in Kowloon Bay since 2019, and specially set up a "lactation room" for female employees to provide a comfortable and relaxing private space to meet the special needs of working mothers. We also cultivate a positive public attitude towards breastfeeding in the workplace, so that breastfeeding mothers can continue to do so. We hope to add more measures to care for employees and increase employees' sense of belonging to the company.
