As we enter the year of the Tiger, I wish every colleague a happy new year, good health and a joyful time at work.

The incessant recycling of spring, summer, autumn and winter implicates that conforming to nature is the best pattern. Spring marks the key moment when the foundation for the coming year is forged. Whether it is an individual entity or an organization, the only way to shorten the distance between our goal and us is by drafting a plan and fulfilling it step by step. We must bear in mind that the things learned and the experience gained in the process will provide plentiful nourishment in the future.

On the cover of this spring issue, we would like to express our gratitude towards the management team in taking the photos that are full of the new year’s atmosphere so that our spring issue can exude a celebratory aura. In this issue’s cover story, we are honoured to have the senior management of DCH China to accept our invitation for exclusive interviews. In the interview, they will share the prospects and plans of China motor and food in 2022, how to embody the concepts of DCH’s services, how to turn crisis into opportunities during the pandemic and how to utilize digitalisation in transformation and to produce synergy between businesses in the “Project Blue Ocean”.

Although we are not able to meet up with colleagues in the mainland and overseas as we please at this moment, our bonds remain unbreakable. The editor of this issue even reached out to DCH’s colleagues stationing at different economies all across Asia to collect photos for celebrating the Chinese lunar new year. This enabled us to send our blessings to one another irrespective of geographical boundaries.

The unique status of printed publications is what we have the most pride in. The weight of the DCH Connect you are now holding reflects the efforts contributed by our editing team to every single page of this newsletter to a certain extent. However, it has also come to our realization that digitalisation has conquered an indispensable part of our daily life. As such, to better present every single business units and department of DCH to our readers in a more comprehensive and stereoscopic way, DCH Connect is not only available as a printed version in traditional Chinese, it is also available as a page-flipping version in traditional and simplified Chinese on the intranet. Besides, it can be viewed on the intranet in traditional and simplified Chinese, and English. This allows more colleagues stationing at different locations to browse the newsletter in various languages and develop a deeper understanding of the future movements and professional areas of DCH as well as the core value of our company.

As the new year took a fresh start, we look into the future and wish all our colleagues in different locations a blissful life and may all your wishes be granted. We look forward to the day when we can finally reunite with one another.

We hope that you like this spring issue and please feel free to contact us should you have any opinions or suggestions.
