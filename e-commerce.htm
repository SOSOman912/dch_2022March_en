Celebrated HOME+ 1st Anniversary with Refreshed Branding
Supercharging to become THE e-shopping destination for consumers in Hong Kong

As early as November 2020, DCH, working together with Hong Kong Broadband Network (HKBN) and other powerful partners, officially launched the HOME+ online shopping platform. Despite the fierce market competition, Home+ has rapidly built awareness and member base in the Hong Kong market in the past year. As part of its ongoing growth evolution, Home+ has launched a refreshed branding that includes a vibrant new logo and colour scheme, family-friendly mascots, and importantly, a refreshed brand tagline of “Always Deliver More”. While these are significant changes, Home+’s core ethos remains the same, just supercharged its mission to become THE destination for consumers by promising more valuable choices, more care & commitment, and more personalised experiences.

New slogan: Always Deliver More

  • Positioning: Target young families and fully tap the over 1 million HKBN's customer base

  • Partners:Home+ continue to build strong partnerships with brands, merchants, retailers and various service providers to offer customers better product & service offerings and more convenient services such as more than 1,000 self-pickup points, with more convenient and faster logistics services being brought to every household.

  • Products:HOME+ has introduced more than 800 brands and over 20,000 products, many of which are international brands, to provide customers with more diverse choices.

  • Quick-Commerce:In addition to asking for a variety of choices, customers are demanding "fast" delivery service. Home+ and DCH Foodmart have jointly offer "100-minute delivery service" to meet customers' needs.

Building out DCH’s New Retail e-Distribution Strategy

Third Party Platforms

Beside HOME+, the e-commerce department will lead the strategic collaboration with third-party online platforms, such as HKTVmall, foodpanda and Ztore, etc., to drive brand exposure and traffic for DCH brands. Not only can these third-party platforms enhance sales and broaden the reach of DCH brands, but also attract more new customers more effectively. Furthermore, through consolidating all DCH brands into a ONE-DCH store on these platforms, we create synergy that can benefit all BU and brands with increased traffic and sales.

DCH Owned Platform

In order to enhance the long-term competitiveness of DCH, the e-commerce department will lead the development of a new middle platform to build a more robust, efficient and flexible technology platform which all BU can leverage to build their e-commerce business and develop a ToC online business. DCH Foodmart will be the first to relaunch their e-commerce site on this new platform and others will follow suit in this year. Stay tuned for a new and improve online shopping experience from DCH Foodmart.
