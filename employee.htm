DCH & Auriga’s Blood Donation Day

DCH and Auriga held the blood donation days in the offices located in Kowloon Bay and Kwai Chung in January this year and December last year respectively. About 80 colleagues successfully donated blood, showing the spirit of selfless dedication. The event was successfully held thanks to the enthusiastic blood donation from all colleagues and their contributions to the Hong Kong Red Cross. We take this opportunity to express our heartfelt thanks to every enthusiastic colleague. We look forward to your participation in the next blood donation day and to join in the good deeds.

The DCH Farmers

In order to allow our colleagues to experience the fun associated with planting, the Corporate Communications Department and the Group Services Department jointly organized the "The DCH Farmers", placing planting pots on the roof of our building in Kowloon Bay and inviting colleagues to form a "DCH Farm" by themselves. The program attracted nearly 40 teams, with over 100 people signing up, and the responses were overwhelming. The planting period started at the end of last year, and the harvest has been obtained, giving colleagues a taste of the joy of farming in the city.

DCH Steps Challenge

In November last year, the Corporate Communications Department specially held the "DCH Steps Challenge" to encourage colleagues to exercise more so as to maintain physical and mental health. In addition to the individual awards, we also added a team award, hoping that colleagues can form a team to "synchronize in walking" in their spare time to enhance team spirit. During the two-week competition, in addition to the daily multi-stair walk, the teams also went out for excursions in the countryside in their spare time in an attempt to increase the number of steps.

This competition attracted more than 50 teams to participate. In the end, the Motor business won the "Most Active Participation Award" and "Department’s Overall Champion" with 20 participating teams and over 9.74 million steps; and the overall champion team was Auriga's team. Mr Ko Wing Kam of Auriga won the "Individual Walking King" with more than 370,000 steps.

Baduanjin Online Workshop

The fifth wave of the pandemic is severe. In order to equip colleagues with a stronger body to fight against the pandemic, the Corporate Communications Department organized an online course and invited Mr Sun Guiping, a senior master in Baduanjin, to teach colleagues how to practise Baduanjin and answer your questions concerning Baduanjin on the spot. The online course attracted more than 30 colleagues to participate, allowing everyone to stretch their limbs, strengthen joints and soothe their minds and body while working in the office or at home.

DCH Group and DCH Food Mart
Credited by “Caring Company”

DCH has been actively fulfilling its social responsibilities and has been awarded the "Caring Company" logo for the 15th consecutive year, while DCH Food Mart has also been awarded for the 14th consecutive year, in recognition of our dedication to giving back to the society over the years.

As a responsible company, DCH has been caring for the society for many years. For example, the DCH’s volunteering team has cooperated with the Hong Kong Red Cross to regularly hold "blood donation activities" at the DCH headquarters, fulfilling its civic obligations in donating blood to help others; in addition, we participated in the "Flag Raising Activities for Fundraising” held by large charitable organizations, inspiring colleagues to participate in more charitable activities and feel the happiness from performing good deeds. We also joined hands with the CITIC Hong Kong and Macau Staff Association to participate in activities that cared for the community, visiting grassroots and elderly families, distributing lucky bags, providing basic necessities and anti-epidemic supplies such as rice, oil and masks, so as to disseminate messages of caring for the community.

DCH has always been committed to providing customers with quality products and services. At the same time, it also shoulders the mission of promoting sustainable social development and conveys love with actions. We would like to express our heartfelt thanks to the nomination from the Hong Kong Lutheran Social Service. In the future, we will continue to work with people from different sectors and social service organizations to further practise corporate social responsibility and work together to care for the community.

Visit to the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA) Hong Kong Garrison Exhibition Center

In order to enhance colleagues' understanding of our country and the concept of our nation, the CITIC Hong Kong and Macau Staff Association specially arranged for employees of CITIC's offices in Hong Kong to visit the PLA Hong Kong Garrison Exhibition Center. Colleagues from DCH also took this rare opportunity to participate enthusiastically.

The PLA Hong Kong Garrison Exhibition Centre opened in July last year. Through different themed exhibition halls, colleagues could experience the 100-year history of the Chinese Communist Party up close, and have a deep understanding of the development of the country and the construction of the Hong Kong Garrison, which broadened their horizons. Participants hope to join in similar activities in the future to increase their awareness of our country.

Happy Friday

In order to welcome the Chinese lunar new year, the Corporate Communications Department specially invited calligraphers to improvise a calligraphy for colleagues, and presented them to colleagues in the dining hall on the 5th floor of DCH Headquarters, wishing everyone a prosperous Year of the Tiger. In addition to the traditional fai chun, calligraphers and teachers tailor made personalized fai chun for each colleague at their request. About 150 colleagues came and more than 300 pieces of fai chun were distributed on that day, to the satisfaction of all colleagues.
